Scouting Report

The Clear Creek Wildcats are the district's most complete team, ranking #1 in District 24-6A at 4-2 with a 16-6 overall record. They are a multi-dimensional threat built around elite pitching depth, elite on-base skills, and disruptive speed. The Wildcats have the district's deepest pitching staff — six arms with ERAs under 2.00 — headlined by Zach Clark (11.9 K/9, 1.74 ERA) who doubles as one of the offense's most dangerous hitters at .354/.407/.521. Theo Prappas is their most dangerous offensive weapon, posting an elite .514 OBP with 10 stolen bases. This is a veteran, well-coached squad that wins with pitching, speed, and fundamental execution.
