The Process

How We Scout

Every report on this site is built from raw box score data — at-bats, hits, strikeouts, walks, earned runs — compiled game by game across the full season. No guesswork. No subjective rankings without the numbers to back them up.

Individual player cards include scouting grades (S through D) across five tools: Contact, Power, Plate Discipline, Speed, and Overall offensive value — all calculated from real production data relative to the district pool.

As more teams are added, the District Leaders section will update automatically to show the top performers across all 8 programs.

What's Tracked

Stat Glossary

  • PA — Plate Appearances (AB + BB + HBP + SF)
  • AVG — Batting Average (H ÷ AB)
  • OBP — On-Base Percentage
  • SLG — Slugging Percentage (TB ÷ AB)
  • OPS — On-Base + Slugging (overall offense)
  • K% — Strikeout Rate (SO ÷ PA)
  • ERA — Earned Run Average (ER ÷ IP × 9)
  • WHIP — Walks + Hits per Inning Pitched
  • K/9 — Strikeouts per 9 Innings
  • 1B · 2B · 3B · HR — Hit type breakdown
  • SB — Stolen Bases (speed metric)
